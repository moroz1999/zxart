PT Sans is the body face used everywhere on zxart.ee. It has six size tiers (11/13/14/18/21/23 px) and only two weights — Regular and Bold. Italic is not used.

Inline links like "Robocop" by Tim Follin use --link-color. The alternate yellow link view all releases is reserved for warning-tone CTAs and emphasis.

Caption — used for dates, parties, and dense secondary metadata under cards.
